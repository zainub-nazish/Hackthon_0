## Tone & Voice

- **Expert** — write from authority, not aspiration
- **Helpful first** — lead with insight, not promotion
- **Conversational** — short sentences, plain language
- **Non-salesy** — CTAs must be low-friction (see below)
- **Honest** — no hype, no vague claims

**Avoid:** buzzwords ("synergy", "disruptive", "game-changing"), passive voice,
walls of text, aggressive sales language.

---

## Post Format Rules

```
Hook     (1–2 lines) — stop the scroll; pose a question or bold claim
[blank line]
Value    (2–4 short paragraphs) — insight, tip, or story
[blank line]
Proof    (1 line) — specific outcome or credibility signal
[blank line]
CTA      (1 line) — one low-friction ask
[blank line]
Hashtags (3–5 max, relevant)
```

- **Length:** 150–250 words (LinkedIn optimal range)
- **Line breaks:** one blank line between each section (LinkedIn renders them)
- **Hashtags:** place at the very end, choose from the pool below

## Topic Rotation (day of week)

The AI rotates topics so the feed stays fresh. One post per calendar day max.

| Day | Topic angle | Notes |
|-----|------------|-------|
| Monday | **Services spotlight** | highlight one specific service with a use-case |
| Tuesday | **Tip / quick insight** | practical, immediately actionable |
| Wednesday | **Case study / outcome** | anonymised client story, specific numbers |
| Thursday | **Process / behind-the-scenes** | how we work, methodology, approach |
| Friday | **Question for engagement** | ask audience something relevant, spark comments |
| Saturday | **Industry observation** | trend or news-hook relevant to audience |
| Sunday | **Motivational / culture** | team values, why we do what we do |

## CTA Library (rotate these)

Use one per post. Never repeat the same CTA two posts in a row.

- "What's your experience with this? Comment below."
- "Agree or disagree? I'd love to hear your take."
- "Send me a message if this resonates — happy to chat."
- "Follow for more [topic] insights every week."
- "What would you add? Drop it in the comments."
- "Curious how this applies to your business? Let's connect."
